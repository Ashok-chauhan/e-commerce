Last Updated: 1/12/2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information for order processing, customer service, and improving user experience.

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal details:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping & billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party gateways such as Razorpay do not store your card details)

    • 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

    When you visit Swagly.in, we automatically collect:

  • Maintain user sessions

    We use the collected information to:
